What is STAKEBUS?

STAKEBUS is one of the first validator nodes of VIREL.

We strongly believe in the need for decentralization, and with this project we want to do our part. If you believe in decentralization and want to participate by ensuring the integrity of the network, this is your place.

STAKEBUS doesn't want to stop here. Once it starts rolling on the blockchain, we don't want it to stop. We will continue working to take STAKEBUS to other places where we can provide support, but that is still to come.
