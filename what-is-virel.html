What is VIREL?

Virel Blockchain is the world’s first MiniDAG, a novel system that simulates a Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) on a linear blockchain. This innovative approach combines the scalability benefits of DAGs with the security and simplicity of a linear blockchain. Key features include:
  • MiniDAG Architecture: Enables increased block throughput and security, with sub-second block times and minimal orphan rates.
  • Merge-Mining & Multi-Chain Security: Secured by multi-chain Proof-of-Work via merge-mining, making it resistant to centralization and efficient in resource use.
  • Performance: Produces a new block every 15 seconds, ensuring rapid transaction processing and network responsiveness.
  • Decentralization & Fair Launch: Launched with no premine, using an ASIC-resistant mining algorithm (RandomVirel), which is CPU-friendly and encourages broad participation.
  • Open-Source & Community-Driven: Developed in Go, fully open-source, and focused on long-term sustainability, with plans for smart contracts and hybrid Proof-of-Stake.

Virel aims to deliver a platform that prioritizes security, scalability, and decentralization, positioning itself as a unique player in the blockchain space
